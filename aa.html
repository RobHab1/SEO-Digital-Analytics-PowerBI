Project Overview

This project was developed for a private client to deliver a Business Intelligence solution for SEO and digital marketing analytics. Company information and business data have been anonymized to protect client confidentiality.

The objective was to transform raw digital marketing data into meaningful business insights through interactive dashboards, KPI reporting and dimensional data modeling in Microsoft Power BI.

The solution documents the complete BI workflow, from data preparation and modeling to dashboard design and executive reporting.

Business Challenge

The client required a centralized reporting solution to monitor website performance, search visibility, traffic quality and marketing effectiveness.

Website PerformanceMonitor visits and platform usage over time.
SEO VisibilityTrack search positions and keyword performance.
Traffic QualityAnalyze bounce rate and engagement trends.
ConversionMeasure how digital activity supports business outcomes.

Data Preparation

Power Query was used to prepare the data for analysis. The ETL process focused on data quality, consistency and reporting readiness.

  • Imported and structured source data
  • Standardized dates and numeric formats
  • Filtered irrelevant and internal records
  • Handled missing values
  • Created calculated columns and business KPIs
  • Validated the final dataset before reporting

Data Model

The analytical model follows a dimensional star schema. A central fact table stores measurable events and keys, while dimension tables support filtering and analysis.

Fact TableTraffic, bounce rate, SEO position and conversion measures.
Date DimensionTime-based analysis and monthly reporting.
Customer DimensionSegment and regional analysis.
Website DimensionPage and platform analysis.
SEO DimensionKeyword and search visibility analysis.
Traffic Source DimensionChannel and acquisition analysis.

Dashboard Features

  • Monthly website traffic analysis
  • Platform and device comparison
  • Bounce rate monitoring
  • SEO position trend analysis
  • Traffic source comparison
  • Conversion KPI tracking
  • Geographic visualization
  • Interactive filters for month, segment and keyword

Business Value

The dashboard enables stakeholders to:

  • Monitor digital performance from one centralized view
  • Identify changes in website traffic and SEO visibility
  • Compare marketing channels and platform usage
  • Evaluate visitor behaviour and conversion performance
  • Reduce manual reporting effort
  • Support faster, data-driven decisions
